About Us

Longhorn Gaming, formerly known as Texas e-Sports Association (TeSPA), is the largest gaming organization at The University of Texas at Austin. We provide a community for students to let out their passion for video games, e-sports, and gaming all under one gigantic roof. From popular MOBAs such as League of Legends and Dota 2 to shooters like Overwatch and Counter-Strike: Global Offensive and even console games such as Super Smash Brothers and Call of Duty, Longhorn Gaming brings all types of video gamers together. Our goal is to provide opportunities for members to accept their gaming side and share their experiences and passions with like minded individuals.
